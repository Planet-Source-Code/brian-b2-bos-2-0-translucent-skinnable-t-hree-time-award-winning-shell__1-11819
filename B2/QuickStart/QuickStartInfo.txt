Use QuickStart to access frequentlly used programs,
such as Internet Explorer or Outlook.

To activate a program in the QuickStart
tray, just click it.

To add a program to the QuickStart tray, just drag its icon to the tray.

To delete an item, right click its icon and select "delete"

You can also add programs to your Quickstart tray by
right clicking the tray and selecting "Open QuickStart Folder".
Then drag the program to the folder.
